FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to an emergency repair?
+
For genuine home emergencies (flooding, electrical hazards, gas issues), our team aims to respond within 1–2 hours. For standard same-day requests, we typically arrive within 4–6 hours of your call.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
+
Absolutely. Every Fix Fortress technician holds the appropriate trade licenses for their specialty, is fully insured, and has passed rigorous background checks. You'll always know who is coming to your home.
Do you offer a warranty on your work?
+
Yes — all labor comes with our 90-day workmanship guarantee. If any issue arises directly related to our work within that period, we return and correct it at no additional charge.
How does the annual maintenance plan work?
+
Our annual plan includes scheduled visits throughout the year to inspect, service, and maintain key home systems — HVAC filters, plumbing checks, roof inspection, pest prevention prep, and more. You'll receive a comprehensive home health report after each visit.
Can I get a quote before committing to a service?
+
Of course. We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all services. Contact us via the form, email, or phone and one of our specialists will assess your needs and provide a detailed, transparent estimate with no hidden fees.
